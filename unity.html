DMA ART 107

Collaborative Group Final Project

Student's were tasked to perform a final collaborative project for Art 107 to design and build an inhabitable virtual world. Working with their groups, they created a navigable open sandbox environment that is compelling and meaningful to its virtual inhabitants through a process of creative ideation, design, planning and content development to completion using the Unity Real-Time Development Environment.

The world they created became the world you as the viewer inhabits, so students were invited to closely consider the kind of world they wanted to create together. They addressed the aesthetic, interactive, and environmental dynamics of their worlds by asking each other - will our world invite exploration and interaction? If so, why?

Teams maintained creative freedom to design and to build any kind of world they wish. Although they were presented with unlimited possibilities, they needed to focus on the necessary assets and components needed to achieve a world that is creative and compelling for its inhabitants and is the result of the shared vision of their groups.
